War Report for Clash of Clans

War Report is the ultimate companion app for Clash of Clans, which offers detailed statistics about every part of the game.

Features

Clans

Monitor your clan's performance and gain valuable insights about your opponents, such as their unlocked super troops and hero levels.

Players

Access comprehensive player profiles and statistics on each player's clan war and legend league performance.

Clan Wars

Keep track of all ongoing clan wars and view detailed information about past wars in the war log.

Clan War League

View every clan's group standings, round results, player rankings, individual clan and player stats, and the total number of (bonus) medals earned.
