      Terms and abbreviations in the game

      Terms and abbreviations in the game

      Term Definition
      Alt Alternate account of a player
      BH Builder Hall
      CC Clan Castle
      CG Clan Games
      Charge One or more heroes are sent deep into the base to destroy key defenses or cc
      Coco loon Single Balloon sent ahead to trigger seeking air mines before main air troops or Healers
      CW Clan War
      CWL Clan War League
      DE Dark Elixir
      Dip Higher Town Hall attacking a lower Town Hall in war
      DPS Damage per second
      Engineering Keeping some defenses low or missing to reduce war weight while upgrading offense
      EQ Earthquake
      F2P Free‑to‑play player who spends no real money
      Farm (defence) Cluster of identical defenses such as Tesla farm or X‑Bow farm
      Farm(ing) Playing mainly to gain resources instead of trophies
      FC Friendly Challenge
      Funnel(ling) Removing outside buildings so troops or heroes walk into the base instead of around it
      Gemmer Player who progresses quickly by spending real money (gems)
      Global chat Removed worldwide chat channel that previously matched random players and helped find clans
      HP Hit points; total health of a troop or building
      Island Defense surrounded by empty space so troops can’t reach it
      Kill squad Small group of troops and heroes used to remove enemy Queen, Clan Castle, or key defenses at the beginning of an attack
      Mirror Attacking the enemy base with the same map position number as your own in war
      Nerf Balance change that makes a troop, spell, or defense weaker
      Push Attacking to gain trophies and climb leagues
      Rushing Upgrading the Town Hall before heroes, troops, buildings and walls are fully upgraded
      Scout Attack used to reveal traps, pathing, and CC troops of a base
      Skelly Donut Skeleton or bat spells with rage and invisibility to destroy multiple defenses and clan castle in the core of the base
      Swag Dropping unnecessary spells/troops at the end of a guaranteed 3‑star to show overkill
      Tag Unique ID starting with # used to identify a specific clan or player
      Teaser base Base that exposes the Town Hall or major value to “bait” attackers into a trap‑heavy side
      TH Town Hall
      Tile Basic distance unit used for ranges and building sizes
      Troll Tesla Hidden Tesla placed in an unexpected corner or outside tile to waste time or surprise attackers
      WB Wall Breaker
      Walk Queen and / or Grand Warden with Healers clearing mostly outside buildings and creating a funnel
      War weight ”Strength” value of a base used for war matchmaking, driven by defenses, heroes, and troops
