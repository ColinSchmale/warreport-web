      THE ULTIMATE COMPANION
      FOR CLASH OF CLANS

      Clan War, CWL and Legend League statistics

      Unlock the full potential of your clan with War Report, the highest rated companion app for Clash of Clans on Google Play and the App Store that provides detailed statistics about every part of the game. Whether you are managing Clan Wars, climbing the leaderboard of the Legends League, or aiming for a promotion of your clan in the Clan War League. War Report equips you with the tools to always stay one step ahead of your opponents.

      The app is completely free to use and adheres to the Safe and Fair Play Policy of the game by only using data from the official Clash of Clans API.

      Features

      Learn more about the features War Report has to offer.

      Clans
      Monitor your clan’s performance and gain valuable insights about your opponents, such as their unlocked super troops and hero levels.
      Players
      Access comprehensive player profiles and statistics on each player’s clan war and legend league performance.
      Clan Wars
      Check ongoing clan wars of all your accounts on a single page. The app also provides detailed information about past wars in the war log.
      Clan War League
      View every clan’s group standings, round results, player rankings, individual clan and player stats, and the total number of (bonus) medals earned.
      Legend League
      Keep track of your performance in the Legend League and compare it with your clan members and friends.
